Scholar Answer
Is it true that whoever sleeps after Asr loses his mind?
WEAK / FABRICATED ضعيف / موضوع

The hadith states:

«مَنْ نَامَ بَعْدَ الْعَصْرِ فَاخْتُلِسَ عَقْلُهُ فَلَا يَلُومَنَّ إِلَّا نَفْسَهُ»

This hadith is classified as ضعيف جداً (very weak) or موضوع (fabricated), as mentioned by Al-Albani.

Scholars have universally agreed that attributing this to the Prophet (ﷺ) is unreliable and acts based on this specific penalty should not be considered binding.

Silsilat al-Ahadith ad-Da'ifah
