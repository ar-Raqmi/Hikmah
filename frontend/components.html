5. Success State Card (`#state-success`)

VERIFIED

What is the ruling on seeking knowledge?

ما فضل طلب العلم؟

طَلَبُ الْعِلْمِ فَرِيضَةٌ عَلَى كُلِّ مُسْلِمٍ. مَنْ سَلَكَ طَرِيقًا يَلْتَمِسُ فِيهِ عِلْمًا سَهَّلَ اللَّهُ لَهُ بِهِ طَرِيقًا إِلَى الْجَنَّةِ.

❧

According to the Sahih al-Bukhari, the Prophet (peace be upon him) said: 'Seeking knowledge is obligatory upon every Muslim.' This hadith emphasizes the foundational importance of education in Islam.

Key Takeaways:

  • Knowledge is a duty for all believers.
  • Both men and women are equally encouraged to learn.
  • وَاللَّهُ أَعْلَمُ (And Allah knows best).
"He who treads a path in search of knowledge, Allah will direct him to a path leading to Paradise." (Sahih Muslim 2699)
Source Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim
